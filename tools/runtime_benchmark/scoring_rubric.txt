Each prompt can give 0, 2, 7, or 10 points
it's basically:

0: complete jibberish
2: catastrophic failure (repeated words, jibberish words)
7: sound grammar, but strange strange content
10: no issues

Additional criterion: Do NOT use answer length as a scoring signal. A longer answer does not earn a higher score and a shorter answer does not earn a lower score. Score solely on correctness, grammar, and factual accuracy — a concise correct answer is a 10 and a verbose answer with errors is still a 2 or 7.
